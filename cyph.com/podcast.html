What is Cyph?

Encryption is hard, but it shouldn’t be. We believe that everyone deserves privacy, not only security experts.

Cyph is a next-generation messenger app that delivers cryptography to the masses, making privacy the new status quo.

It was founded by two former SpaceX engineers and childhood friends to meet their own high standards of what chat should be: completely private and delightfully dope.

With Cyph, never again will you need to ask someone else to install or configure or sign up for anything, when all you need is a one-off private discussion.

Just one click and you’re secure.
